A1.4 Legal Analysis (ERA s.13 & s.23)

ERA 1996 s.13 - Unlawful Deductions: An employer shall not make deductions from wages unless:

  1. Authorised by written contract, OR
  2. Authorised by statute, OR
  3. Agreed in writing by the employee.

Application to this case:

  • No contractual clause: Employment contract (CP-01) contains NO clause authorising deduction for equipment loss/damage.
  • No statutory authority: No statute authorises employer to deduct equipment values from wages.
  • No written agreement: Claimant did NOT agree to deduction. Employer unilaterally "declared" values and threatened deduction.

Conclusion: Any deduction attempted (or threatened) violates ERA s.13 and ERA s.23 (restriction on contracting out of rights).
